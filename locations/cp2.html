Coffee & Chocolate | Knoxville, TN

Coffee and Chocolate, a beloved establishment in Knoxville, is a true haven for connoisseurs and enthusiasts alike. With a delightful array of delectable cakes, indulgent chocolates, tantalizing cookies, and an impressive selection of coffees, it is a destination that delights the senses.

Immerse yourself in a world of flavors as you savor the richness and complexity of their meticulously crafted creations. Each bite reveals a symphony of tastes and textures, a testament to the artistry and passion that goes into every handmade treat.

Beyond the culinary delights, Coffee and Chocolate offers more than just an exquisite menu. It provides a sanctuary where one can unwind, find respite, and engage in productive endeavors. Whether seeking solace while working or simply desiring a moment of tranquility, the ambiance is tailored to nurture a sense of relaxation and focus.

Situated in the heart of Knoxville, this haven also affords the opportunity to witness the vibrant energy of Market Square. As you savor your delectable treats and sip on a perfectly brewed cup of coffee, you can immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of this bustling hub, embracing the dynamic tapestry of life unfolding before you.
