The Golden Roast | Knoxville, TN

The Golden Roast lives up to its name by offering an extensive selection of their own freshly roasted coffee blends, showcasing their dedication to the art of coffee. However, their creativity doesn't stop there. Prepare to embark on a captivating journey as you explore their intriguing espresso drink menu, which pushes the boundaries of flavor combinations.

Are you in the mood for something bold and unexpected? Try the "Big Orange Mocha," a tantalizing blend that combines the rich notes of chocolate with a burst of citrusy orange, creating a harmonious balance of flavors. It's a delightful twist on a classic favorite that will awaken your taste buds.

or those seeking a taste of tropical paradise, the "Ghirardelli Chocolate and Coconut Cold Brew" offers a refreshing escape. Picture the creamy smoothness of Ghirardelli chocolate mingling with the tropical essence of coconut, all infused into a chilled and invigorating cold brew. It's a true indulgence that transports you to a sunny beachside getaway.

Now, brace yourself for a truly intriguing concoction that will pique your curiosity: the "Seaside Sunrise." Combining the unexpected duo of pineapple juice and blue curacao with the boldness of espresso, this drink is an adventurous blend of sweet and tangy flavors. Let the tropical sweetness of pineapple dance with the vibrant essence of blue curacao, perfectly balanced by the rich, aromatic kick of espresso. It's a unique experience that defies expectations and leaves a lasting impression.

At The Golden Roast, each sip is an opportunity to embark on a sensory exploration, where innovative flavor combinations and expertly crafted coffee creations take center stage. Whether you opt for their signature roasts or venture into the realm of imaginative espresso drinks, prepare to be delighted by the artistry and inventiveness that awaits you at this remarkable coffee destination.
